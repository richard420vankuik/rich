Woolworths: Back our Bees

Bees are responsible for 1 in every 3 bites of food we take, yet the health and future of bees remains at risk worldwide.

The goal for this campaign was to create social awareness about bees through the #BackOurBees hashtag using posters, QR Codes, packaging, a microsite and social media posts that provides consumers with knowledge about the issue as well as urge consumers to purchase plants, and products that evidently help bees.

In order to futher communicate the consequences we would face if bees were not around, I refined my poster design to show the friut 'disapearing'. The use of type instead of the banana visually represents that.















Spotify: My City Sound

Spotify was launched in South Africa in April of 2018 and had no awareness in this foreign market. The competition is rather challenging as most south african’s find themselves using iTunes or other music streaming platforms.

#MyCitySound allows for South Africans from all across the country to come together with music and feel united when sharing the love for local South African Music. Resulting in more exposure for the artists, and more music for the listeners.

The launch of this campaign is a Spotify event in Cape Town featuring 7 local artists who play a concert for the people of South Africa - creating exposure and awareness for the artists as well as Spotify SA. After this concert, Outdoor and Digital print ads are released. Each artist who performed at the event has a specialised poster. Following this is the social media campaign. Showing that while Cape Town has AKA and Youngsta, Jozi has Kwesta and Mi Casa. Also, Durban has Shekinah, Black Coffee, and Nasty C.












Jupiter Oils: Brand Activation 2018



For the Vega Brand Activation of 2018, our client was Jupiter Oils, a South African brand that focuses on providing customers with a 100% natural and organic product. The product is locally sourced and wildcraft organic.
I created some infographic posters for the brand, I also created a new website for the brand which they could possibly use in order to create more awareness online.

It was a great experience to be able to work with a real-life client who provides feedback on our work. It was interesting to ideate brand building solutions while designing and crafting the work.






MADDD Labs: Design Jam 2018

"The current school model is based upon the STEM system in which Science, Technolology, Engineering and Maths play the leading roles. This is where ReimagiNation Foundation comes in: the mission of this NPO, formed by visionary Vega founder Gordon Cook, is to redress this imbalance by evolving this reductive acronym to STEAM in which the Arts are placed in the crucial position they deserve." - ReimagiNation Foundation

As a team, decided that the use of South African influencers like Die Antwoord and Jack Parow will catch the target audience's attention and create awareness for the brand. We created a unique brand identity and visual ivdentity for MADDD Labs. We extended the visual identity into a proposal document template, a presentation template, a social media pack, and a MADDD Labs Logo. I designed a prototype website for MADDD Labs that will create necessary awareness around the brand with the use of impactful creative content as well as social media content.











WP Blood Transfusion Service: YouthTube

Many people are scared to donate blood due to the fear of needles, which is a big issue as many people around the world constantly need blood (every 2 seconds). In this video, I wanted to target the youth of South Africa and encourage them to do something remarkable, and donate blood. The fact that simply 1 pint of blood can save up to 3 lives, should urge people to do something 'simple' and basicaly save someones life - it's that easy.
